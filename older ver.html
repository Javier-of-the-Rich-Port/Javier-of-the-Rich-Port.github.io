Okami

A quick overview of a great game.

What is Okami?

Okami is a 2006 Playstation 2 game following the Adventures of Amaterasu through a Japanese Fantasy world drawing inspiration from legend, myth, and folklore.

Released to rave reviews althought poor sales Okami became a cult hit before the year was out, later spawning several ports, a remastering and a sequel.

Story and Characters

Okami follows the adventures of Amaterasu, a Shinto Goddess in the form of a wolf and her Companion Issun through the land of Nippon. Amaterasu while recruiting the aid of mortals, heroes and gods alike must vanquish evil and save the land, helping the people along the way.

Art and Music

The game is rendered in a style reminiscent of Japanese water color and scroll paintings giving it a unique feel and style unseen in other games

The award winning soundtrack is a classical Japanese style played on traditional instruments keeping in theme with the setting and story.

Reviews and Information

Critically acclaimed, Okami received 93/100 from Metacritic and 9.1/10 from IGN as well as rave reviews from fans.
